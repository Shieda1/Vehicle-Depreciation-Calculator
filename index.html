What is the vehicle depriciation?
The vehicle depriciation is the difference between the vehicle's value when it is bought and when it is sold.
How to calculate vehicle depriciation?
To calculate vehicle depriciation you should use the following formula:
vehicle depriciation = value of the vehicle when it's purchased - value of vehicle aftar a specific number of years
Why use vehicle depriciation?
The vehicle depriciation is used in order to know how much does the value of the vehicle has decreased over the years.
